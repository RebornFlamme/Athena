## Tactical plan (as executed)

1. **Attack** (Sector ALPHA): first line via stairwell to fire flat 3B; second line
   4th floor (extension 4B); third line stairwell protection floors 5–8. Interior
   only — courtyard façade has no exterior access.
2. **Rescue** (Sector BRAVO): ladders pick visible window/balcony victims by
   priority (proximity to fire > smoke exposure > vulnerability): 3A → 4A → 5C →
   5A → 6C. Search pairs sweep floor by floor (primary 03:40, secondary 04:20);
   remaining residents sheltered in place, then escorted down with smoke hoods
   once stairwell under control (03:20–03:47).
3. **Water**: tank water first strike; 230 m hose-lay to H-ROUET (charged 03:06);
   second supply from H-PRADO-N (03:22); reserve from H-CASSIS (03:29).
4. **Safety**: RIT/safety team standing by from 02:58 (ENDOUME pair) — committed
   once (MAYDAY 03:11–03:17); BA board managed at CP; exclusion zone 35 m.
5. **Medical** (Sector MEDICAL): CCP on tram platform (−27, −45); triage
   red/yellow/green; noria of 3 medic units + SMUR to La Timone & Hôpital
   Européen; MEDICAL 1 (DSM) coordinates with SAMU regulation.
6. **Command**: COS transfers ENGINE LOUVAIN 1 → GROUP SOUTH (02:55:40) →
   COLUMN 1 (03:04); CP runs SITAC board, BA board, comms log from 03:15.
